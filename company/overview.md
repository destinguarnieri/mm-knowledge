# Money Machine Inc — Company Overview

Money Machine Inc (MM) is a quantitative trading firm and AI research lab built at the intersection of rigorous financial engineering and cutting-edge machine learning.

## What We Do

We deploy systematic, data-driven strategies across liquid markets, combining deep quantitative research with proprietary AI models. Our edge comes from the feedback loop between our trading operations and our research lab — every position we take informs our models, and every model improvement sharpens our execution.

## Research & AI

Our AI research arm isn't a side project — it's core to the firm. We build and maintain in-house models for signal generation, risk estimation, execution optimization, and market microstructure analysis. We move fast, ship to production, and measure everything.

## Culture

We operate like a tech company that happens to trade. Small teams, high ownership, and a bias toward building over buying. If something doesn't exist and we need it, we build it.

## North Star

Find alpha. Keep it. Scale it.
